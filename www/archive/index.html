Tracking the pulse and health of international aid data from donor countries.

Pulse chart

The chart above represents data from the International Aid Transparency Initiative. By monitoring the frequency, quantity and quality of published data from IATI, we aim to show the health, growth and completeness of data across donor countries. By monitoring the state of IATI data, researchers and policy analysts can know when new data is added and how well it represents actual aid flows from donor countries.
